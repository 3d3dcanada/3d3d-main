# 3D3D Marketing System Handoff

## What Is Included
- `seo/keywords.md`: keyword map, intent clusters, gap analysis, and prioritization
- `seo/site-structure.md`: site architecture, page structure, copy blocks, FAQs, links, and technical SEO guidance
- `seo/metadata.md`: page-by-page titles, descriptions, headings, social metadata, and schema
- `marketing/strategy.md`: positioning, conversion copy system, platform strategy, and viral engine
- `marketing/ads.md`: paid ad copy for Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube
- `marketing/social-posts.md`: 60 platform-ready organic posts
- `marketing/video.md`: YouTube titles, thumbnail text, short hooks, and scripts
- `assets/layouts.md`: creative specifications and layout instructions
- `assets/prompts.md`: generation prompts for downstream asset creation
- `assets/produced-assets.md`: inventory of actual rendered assets and how to regenerate them
- `assets/generate_assets.py`: reusable asset generator built on repo-local photography
- `content/calendar.md`: 30-day calendar, repurposing system, workflow, and KPI focus
- `docs/assumptions.md`: assumptions and validation list
- `docs/decisions.md`: strategic decisions made in this package
- `docs/research-sources.md`: source grounding and market conclusions
