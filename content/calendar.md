## Repurposing System
Use one substantial job or case study to create 10 assets:

1. Long-form YouTube case-study video
2. 30-second YouTube Short
3. Instagram Reel with first-failure hook
4. Facebook story post with full context
5. Carousel: part problem -> material choice -> result
6. Static quote graphic using strongest line from the video
7. Blog article or case-study page
8. Email or DM follow-up asset for leads with similar use case
9. FAQ story answering the most common objection from the job
10. Retargeting ad creative using the before/after visual

## Weekly Production Rhythm
- Monday: film or gather the core case-study footage
- Tuesday: edit the long-form video and extract shorts
- Wednesday: publish the authority piece and supporting static asset
- Thursday: publish the conversion post and retargeting creative
- Friday: publish the founder POV or FAQ piece and review results
- Weekend: story polls, community replies, and comment harvesting

## Publishing Workflow

### 1. Creation
- Start from one real job, question, or failure mode
- Capture raw proof: broken part, environment, measurement, material, finished result
- Draft the core narrative in one sentence before editing

### 2. Review
- Check technical accuracy first
- Confirm every caption has one main point and one CTA
- Remove generic filler, intro fluff, and vague “innovation” language

### 3. Publish
- Publish with platform-native formatting
- Keep the link target singular: quote page, service page, or consult page
- Respond to comments in the first hour where possible

### 4. Optimize
- Track saves, shares, click-through rate, watch time, and DM starts
- Promote winners into paid creative
- Turn repeated comments into new FAQ, story, and blog assets

## KPI Focus
- Quote starts
- Contact form submissions
- DM conversations started
- Service-page click-through rate
- YouTube average view duration
- Saves and shares on educational posts
